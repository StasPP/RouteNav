This utility provides GoogleMaps(R) data download for offline use in RouteNave and OZiExplorer/Global Mapper software.

The geo-referenced tiles can be choosed and downloaded before download.

For multi-add use Shift key + left mouse button. Multi-deleting made by Ctrl key.

Zoom levels changed by Shift key + mouse wheel up/down turning. Downloaded tiles can be redacted with tile editor.

Downloading more than 40-50 tiles is not recomended.

GoogleMaps(R) copyrighted by Google and the maps/space images owners.
